About Inferential Analysis

                
This analysis uses machine learning models to make predictions about future video game sales trends:

                
    
                    
  • Linear Regression Model: Predicts future sales based on historical trends
    • 
                    
  • 5-Year Forecasting: Sales predictions for the next 5 years
    • 
                    
  • Trend Analysis: Identification of growth or decline patterns
    • 
                    
  • Model Performance: R-squared scores and accuracy metrics
    • 
                    
  • Statistical Inference: Confidence intervals and significance testing
    • 
                

                

                    
Model Details:

                    
The model aggregates total global sales per year and uses linear regression to identify trends. The R-squared score indicates how well the model explains the variance in the data.
